Our Journey in Energy Innovation

Key milestones that shaped our mission to transform energy infrastructure

2018

Company Founded

Founded by energy industry veterans with a vision to bridge traditional grid infrastructure with intelligent technology solutions.

2019

First Platform Launch

Launched our first demand response platform, serving 12 utility partners and managing 500MW of distributed energy resources.

2020

Grid Analytics Innovation

Introduced AI-powered grid analytics, achieving 23% improvement in load forecasting accuracy for utility partners.

2021

Enterprise Expansion

Expanded to serve enterprise customers, managing energy optimization for Fortune 500 companies and achieving $2.8B in cumulative cost savings.

2022

Renewable Integration

Launched comprehensive renewable energy integration platform, connecting 15.2 TWh of clean energy to the grid.

2023

Global Recognition

Received Energy Innovation Award for Grid Modernization Leadership and achieved SOC 2 certification for enterprise security.

2024

Market Leadership

Serving 847 active utilities across 47 countries, managing 98.7% grid efficiency with 98.9% customer satisfaction rating.

2025 & Beyond

Future Vision
